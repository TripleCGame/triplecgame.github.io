Who is TripleC?

What Have We Done in Web2?​

Triple C was a well-established game publisher in the web2 world, and the founding team has over 10 years of global game publishing experience. We have published, marketed, and promoted over 20 mobile games for major game developers such as NetEase, Tencent, Century Games, UserJoy, and FriendTimes. Each of these games has 5 to 10 million players and a turnover of a few million dollars each month.

We have rich industry experience in all aspects of game publishing, including distribution, operation and services. Our earlier success stems from quality control and marketing strategy. Holding high standards in game selection, we have sharpened our insights on quality games over years of practice. And with a deep understanding of various markets and players' demands, we tailor made marketing strategies to match each geographic location.

What Makes Us Unique in Web3?​

TripleC has a diversified team from complementary backgrounds.

Our tech team is a group of engineers from top game studios and crypto exchanges. We have years of hands-on experience in both game and smart contract development. We bring in our mature Web2 pratice in SDK, API and mobile app, adding to our newly developed streamlined blockchain technology. Our technological capacities span over multiple directions, covering both front end to back end. From architecture design for platform construction, to protocol details for DApp deployment, we have the whole package that delivers Web3 products in a turnkey fashion.

The tokenomic design on TripleC comes from experts in the fields of economy, finance and mathematics. It draws on the successful experience of traditional finance and DeFi, with math models inheriting the advantages of both. TripleC not only designed efficient and sustainable tokenomics for its own platform, but will also design and optimize tokenomics for each game, taking into account the in-game numerical system, together with features in traditional finance and DeFi.

Although technology and tokenomics are our killer skills, what makes TripleC stand out in the world of Web3 is our resources in game publishing industry. The close network we built with top game developers/studios (so called content providers or CP in the gaming industry) gives us exclusive access to quality games. Currently we have 10 high-quality, even AAA level, games in pipeline, ready to be released in 6-8 months. What's more exciting are new games and digital collectibles we are going to create with world famous anime IPs. All the contents are going to be published on our platform, and distributed using our established channels and KOL network. These promotion avenues have been tested on the ground for the past years, and proven to be cost-efficient, practical yet effective.
