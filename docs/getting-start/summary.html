GameFi is$x^2$ the fastest and most commercial entry point for Web3. It is the basic component of Web3, and a test field for stability and future expansion of Metaverse. With a grand market outlook, TripleC dive into the field of Gamefi, backed by rich game resources in $\int{x}dx$ Web2 and a clear technical roadmap in Web3.

TripleC is a Web3 game platform, a publisher and incubator. We strive to eliminate all obstacles standing between blockchain technology and games. We provide a set of turnkey technologies, tools and service solutions, covering the entire cycle of GameFi products. With our DApp, API/SDK and math models, frictionless adaption of blockchain technology in the gaming industry is made possible. Using the infrastructure and services on our platform, game developers could enjoy TripleC as a one-stop shop for tokenomics, NFT and DeFi solutions, in other words, GameFi as a Service (GaaS). Moreover, as a platform with games, wallet and marketplace, we will build an open ecosystem to bring Web2 and Web3 players together, but a closed user cycle to seamlessly convert Web2 players into Web3 players. The whole ecosystem is going to be built on subnets with high TPS (transactions per second) and minimum gas fees. The prosperity of the ecosystem is further sustained and boosted by game communities and derivative content space.

TripleC has a diversified team of experts in games, marketing, economy and finance, from both industry and academics. The team has years of experience in game publishing and has successfully published several best-sellers in Japan. With accessible distribution channels, TripleC is ready to sweep the market soon with 10 high-quality games in pipeline, including AAA level games and world famous anime IPs. We have also designed an innovative and sustainable token (CCCG) for our platform, an ETF model to support token value using the concept of dynamics index and basket.

Standing with worldwide users, TripleC aims to build the most trusted service platform and the most sustainable GameFi ecosystem. Our all-around capacities in content, tokenomics and marketing will create unprecedented synergy in Web3, and are about to bring to the GameFi world more Games, more Fi, and more Players.
